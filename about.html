Our Story

Gan Laboratory is a renowned pathological and clinical chemistry analyzing laboratory established in 1985. For over three decades, we have been serving the community with reliable and accurate diagnostic services.

Located in Barasat, Kolkata, our laboratory is recognized by the Department of Family and Welfare, Government of West Bengal. We are also associated with the National Council of Vocational Research and Training (NCVRT).

Our mission is to provide high-quality diagnostic services at affordable prices, making healthcare accessible to all sections of society. We believe in continuous improvement and have consistently upgraded our facilities with advanced technology and equipment.

Gan Laboratory is not just a diagnostic center but a trusted healthcare partner for thousands of patients across Kolkata and North 24 Parganas. We take pride in our contribution to improving healthcare outcomes through accurate and timely diagnostic services.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality diagnostic services with accuracy, reliability, and affordability, making healthcare accessible to all.

Our Vision

To be the leading diagnostic laboratory in West Bengal, setting benchmarks in quality, service, and innovation.

Director's Desk

Mr. Asim Gan

Director, Gan Laboratory

As an executive member of All India Institute of Medical Technologists (AIIMT), I have been advocating for the development of Medical Technology professionals since 1990. My journey in the field of pathology and laboratory medicine has been dedicated to improving healthcare accessibility and quality in our region.

At Gan Laboratory, we are committed to providing accurate and reliable diagnostic services while maintaining the highest standards of quality and ethics. Our team of qualified professionals works tirelessly to ensure that every patient receives personalized care and accurate results.

I believe that without developing Medical Technology professionals, no improvement in the health care system will be possible. This belief has guided our efforts in training and nurturing talent in the field of laboratory medicine.

I am proud of our achievements over the past three decades, including our contributions to various community health initiatives, rural healthcare programs, and educational activities. We remain dedicated to serving the community with passion and excellence.
