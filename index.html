About Gan Laboratory

Gan Laboratory is a recognized Pathological Laboratory authorized by the Department of Family and Welfare, Government of West Bengal. With over 35 years of experience, we are committed to providing accurate, reliable and timely diagnostic services.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced technology operated by a team of qualified and experienced professionals.
